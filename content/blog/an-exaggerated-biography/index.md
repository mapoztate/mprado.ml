If you're reading this, welcome to the first blog! I started this blog as an alternative to writing a journal. I've found this as an outlet to express myself, improve my writing ability, and help others in situations similar to me in the past. I'll update it periodically - but no commitments! But anyway, let's get started! I was writing my About Me page, and as I started to list off things that people would've loved to know about me, I realized I had a lot of plans in the future; way too many for an About Me and more for just rambling. So I figured, why not just start off my blog like this? An extended version of an About Me page.
